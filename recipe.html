The Perfect Foolproof Pasta

By Iliana Montes de Oca

Description:

A flavourful warm dish with rich noodles lathered
in salted butter and olive oil, topped with shrimp,
a clove of cilantro and sprinkled with a hint of salt
as seen in the picture, with all the fixin's on top.

Inredients:

  • One Bag-Organic Pasta
    Noodles
  • 3 Tablespoons or to Taste-
    Extra Vergin Olive Oil
  • Two to Tree Shrimp per Person-
    Organic Frozen Shrimp
  • A Medium to Small Pan
  • Four to Six Leaves-Basil
  • Several Peices-Cilantro
    (Not Required for Recipe)
  • Salt-to taste
  • 1/8 to 2/8 cup Reccomended-Butter
  • A medium to large sized
    metal pot
  • Enough water to fill the pot 2/3 of the way
  • To Taste-Lemon Pepper (Not required
    but adds a definite
    exquisite taste that you won't get
    without it).

Directions:

    So, what you're going to do is simple and will take little effort. Here are the simple steps:

  1. First, fill up the pot with water and boil it on low heat.
  2. Next, what you're going to do is defrost two of the frozen shrimps by putting them on the counter-still incased in the bag-Two per person.
  3. While the Shrimp is defrosting and the water is boiling, prep the noodles by pulling them out of the bag, and cutting them in half.
  4. Then place them in the boiling water simmering on low heat.
  5. Once you do that, the shrimp should be done defrosting. If they are cool to the touch, then reheat them.
  6. When the pasta is complete cooking, then strain it and put it to the side.
  7. Put the butter and olive oil in the pan and heat until the butter is melted and the solution, (which we will call 'boliv oil') is warm.
  8. Wash cilantro and cut it.
  9. Put the pasta in a large serving bowl, and mix in the Boliv oil, then add the salt and lemon pepper to taste.
  10. Next put the shrimp on top with the cilantro, and serve.

(Serves best warm).
